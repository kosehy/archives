# Summary

This Substack digest argues that engineering authority is not disappearing under AI, but changing shape. As technical troubleshooting becomes less central to status and trust, senior engineers need to build authority through judgment, anticipation, organizational context, and cross-team influence.

The digest uses that theme to connect a wider set of discussions around leadership paths, AI harnesses, enterprise AI adoption, codebase entropy, demand shaping, burnout-resistant team leadership, and cross-disciplinary innovation. The common thread is that engineering leadership now depends less on technical heroics alone and more on systems thinking and coordinated influence.
