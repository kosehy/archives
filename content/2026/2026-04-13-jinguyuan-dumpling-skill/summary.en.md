## One-paragraph summary

This repository presents an AI Skill for Jinguyuan Dumpling Restaurant, a long-running Beijing dumpling shop, exposing restaurant information and practical customer actions through an agent-friendly interface. The skill supports MCP-based queries for location, hours, delivery, dumpling cooking instructions, Wi-Fi, and announcements, and also includes an embedded Meituan queueing component that lets an AI assistant check waiting conditions, issue queue numbers, monitor queue progress, and cancel queue orders. The project is framed as an example of a small local business building a machine-readable service layer for AI assistants.

## Key points

- The skill exposes restaurant information through MCP queries.
- It includes an embedded Meituan queueing workflow for real waiting-list actions.
- The repository is structured as an installable skill for multiple agent IDE environments.
- It positions a local restaurant as a service provider not just for humans, but for AI assistants acting on behalf of users.

## Memorable takeaway

A neighborhood restaurant is packaging its operational interface in a form that AI assistants can directly understand and use.
