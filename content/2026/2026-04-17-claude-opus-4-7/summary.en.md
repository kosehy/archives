# Summary

Anthropic presents Claude Opus 4.7 as a meaningful upgrade over Opus 4.6, especially for advanced software engineering, long-running tasks, high-resolution vision, and instruction following. The announcement also puts unusual emphasis on test-user feedback across coding, agents, code review, multimodal work, and enterprise workflows.

A major part of the release framing is safety. Anthropic says Opus 4.7 is the first model being deployed with automatic safeguards against prohibited or high-risk cybersecurity requests, while keeping Mythos Preview more restricted for now.
