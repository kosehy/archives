# MeteoSimulation | Subway Station Fire and Evacuation Response Service Using Digital Twin Technology (Open Innovation Type)

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: MeteoSimulation
- Demonstration year: 2024
- Support amount: KRW 25,000,000
- Location: 674 Gyeongin-ro, Namdong-gu, Incheon (Ganseok-dong)
- Demonstration partner: Incheon Transit Corporation
- Demonstration target:
  - 2 stations on Incheon Line 1 (Incheon City Hall Station, Dongsu Station)
  - 2 stations on Incheon Line 2 (Incheon Gajwa Station, Seongnam Station)
- Area: Basement levels 3 to 5
- Capacity: Approximately 2,400 people (during peak passenger hours)
- Usage: Transportation facility (station)
- Special note: For Dongmak Station and Incheon Line 1, 3D assets of tunnels and station exterior materials are available

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: Subway Station Fire and Evacuation Response Service Using Digital Twin Technology (Open Innovation Type)
- Purpose: To enhance subway station fire response and evacuation analysis and verify a fire and evacuation response service that can be used from a performance-based design perspective.

## Demonstration Details
- Advanced fire and evacuation response service for subway stations in response to the seriousness of subway station fires and the mandatory adoption of performance-based fire protection design.
1. Analysis and demonstration of subway-station fire and evacuation simulation
2. Analysis of laws and systems related to subway-station fire and evacuation
3. Investigation of evacuation safety methods and safety technologies for subway stations

## Demonstration Objectives
1. 95% accuracy in ASET prediction
2. 95% accuracy in RSET prediction
3. 90% to 95% accuracy in temperature prediction
4. 95% accuracy in visibility-distance prediction
5. Pass/fail result for a performance-based design evaluation report

## Demonstration Method
1. Collected field data related to subway stations and then analyzed subway-station fire and evacuation simulation
   - Calculated available safe egress time and required safe egress time, and evaluated evacuation routes
2. Analyzed characteristics of subway-station fire and evacuation and characteristics of evacuation simulation
3. Conducted review by performance-based fire protection design experts

## Demonstration Results
1. ASET prediction accuracy: 99% / Achieved
2. RSET prediction accuracy: 90% / Achieved
3. Temperature prediction accuracy: 100% / Achieved
4. Visibility-distance prediction accuracy: 100% / Achieved
5. Performance-based design evaluation report: Pass
