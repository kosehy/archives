# CityEyeLab | AI Video Analytics-Based Data Generation Solution for Smart Gas Stations

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: CityEyeLab
- Demonstration year: 2023
- Support amount: KRW 30,000,000
- Location: 942 Bongo-daero, Gyeyang-gu, Incheon (Seoun-dong)
- Demonstration partner: GS Caltex
- Demonstration scope: 24 total sites, including 17 in Incheon and 7 in Gyeonggi (Gimpo, Bucheon, Ansan, and Siheung)
- Category: Data

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: AI Video Analytics-Based Data Generation Solution for Smart Gas Stations
- Purpose: To generate data related to license plates, congestion, movement paths, and safety management in gas-station environments using AI video analytics, and to verify the potential for public and private utilization.

## Demonstration Details
1. Verified ANPR (Auto Number Plate Recognition) accuracy performance
2. Estimated congestion at gas-station car washes using cameras and provided real-time waiting vehicle information to customers
3. Provided conflict-analysis information between vehicles for safety management and monitoring at gas stations

## Demonstration Objectives
1. Analyze and validate license plate (ANPR) recognition accuracy
2. Measure movement paths and dwell-time data
3. Provide conflict-point data for safety monitoring
4. Measure car-wash congestion and provide waiting vehicle information

## Demonstration Method
- Used existing camera infrastructure at gas stations, or installed cameras where necessary, to conduct AI video analysis of vehicles and customers, and then trained on the data to further improve the solution.

## Demonstration Results
- Achieved all objectives
- Successfully attracted investment
- Won the grand prize at the Seonggwak Valley IR pitching competition
- Secured diverse background data and core technologies related to CCTV-based AI video analysis
