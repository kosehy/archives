# GlobalBridge | Data Acquisition and Analysis of Specific Vehicle Entry Status for Safer Urban Traffic Environments

## Basic Information

- Demonstration company: GlobalBridge
- Location: 175 Art Center-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon (Songdo-dong)
- Demonstration partner: IFEZ
- Demonstration resources: CCTV and streetlights within the urban area of the Incheon Free Economic Zone
- Category: Infrastructure / Platform
- Demonstration method: IoT Demonstration Support Project (demonstration-cost support type)

## Demonstration Overview

- Case name: Acquisition and analysis of effective data on specific-vehicle entry into urban areas to ensure a safer traffic environment and provide useful data for local governments and public institutions
- Purpose: To analyze the status of specific vehicles entering urban areas and secure practical data that can be used by public institutions.
- Usage context: Urban traffic safety, city-data collection, and public-sector-oriented analytics

## Demonstration Target and Environment

- Utilized CCTV and streetlight infrastructure in the urban area of the Incheon Free Economic Zone
- On-site installation photos at urban intersections and vehicle traffic areas are available

## Currently Confirmed Information

- Location, demonstration partner, demonstration company, category, demonstration method, and on-site images confirmed
