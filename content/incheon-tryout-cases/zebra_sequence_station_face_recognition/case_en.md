# Zebra&Sequence | Building a Safety Network at Incheon National University Entrance Station Through a Pedestrian Face Recognition SW System

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: Zebra&Sequence
- Location: Underground 155 Incheontower-daero, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon (Songdo-dong)
- Demonstration partner: Incheon Transit Corporation
- Demonstration target: Facilities on Incheon Subway Lines 1, 2, and 7
- Usage: Transportation facilities (tunnels and bridges), restrooms, etc.
- Major demonstration note: A restroom occupancy information alert function was completed at Incheon National University Entrance Station
- Category: Space

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: Building a Safety Network at Incheon National University Entrance Station Through a Pedestrian Face Recognition SW System (early missing-person detection and crime prevention)
- Purpose: To build a safety network at Incheon National University Entrance Station through a face-recognition-based detection system for early missing-person detection and crime prevention.

## Demonstration Method
- Linked the AI-based moving-object face recognition software system with existing intelligent CCTV installed in the station (moving walkway at Exit 4 and 2 cameras in the concourse) to verify recognition and detection rates for target individuals such as fare evaders.
- Donated to Incheon Transit Corporation after the demonstration.
