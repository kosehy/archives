# CityEyeLab | AI-Based Real-Time Traffic Information Monitoring Platform Demonstration

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: CityEyeLab
- Location: 929 Wondang-daero, Seo-gu, Incheon (Wondang-dong)
- Demonstration partner: Incheon Urban Development Corporation
- Demonstration target: Intersection in front of the Geomdan Project Division of Incheon Urban Development Corporation
- Category: Space

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: AI-Based Real-Time Traffic Information Monitoring Platform Demonstration
- Purpose: To use intersection video data to predict traffic information in real time and measure automatic vehicle detection rates, thereby validating the business model.

## Demonstration Method
- Installed cameras at six intersections in Geomdan New Town and used received data to predict real-time traffic information and measure automatic detection rates for passing vehicles in order to validate the business model.
