# TLabs | VPS Technology Demonstration for Spaces and Facilities in Incheon International Airport Terminals 1 and 2

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: TLabs
- Demonstration year: 2023
- Support amount: KRW 30,000,000
- Location: 271 Gonghang-ro, Jung-gu, Incheon (Unseo-dong)
- Demonstration partner: Incheon International Airport Corporation
- Demonstration target: Spaces and facilities in Incheon International Airport Terminals 1 and 2
  - Details: Terminals 1 and 2, access roads, and facilities such as parking lots
  - Intended use: Digital transformation of airport spaces and facilities

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: Demonstration of VPS technology capable of estimating highly accurate user positions within a few tens of centimeters in Incheon International Airport Terminals 1 and 2 using a smartphone, at a level suitable for commercial service
- Field: Precise indoor/outdoor positioning, VPS, digital transformation, airport space services
- Purpose: To verify commercially viable technology that allows airport users to estimate their precise location inside Terminals 1 and 2 using smartphones.

## Demonstration Details
- Demonstration of technology enabling Incheon International Airport users to estimate their precise location within a few tens of centimeters in Terminals 1 and 2 via smartphone, at a commercially usable level.

## Demonstration Objectives
- Required visual information volume: 5 sec
- Initial positioning accuracy: 90%

## Demonstration Method
- Collected data on the 3rd floor of Incheon International Airport Terminal 2 using a LiDAR-based scanner and demonstrated VPS algorithm performance.

## Demonstration Results
- Required visual information volume: 3.9 sec (achieved)
- Initial positioning accuracy: 90% (achieved)
