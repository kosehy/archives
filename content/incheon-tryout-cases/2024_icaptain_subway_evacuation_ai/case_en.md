# iCaptain | AI-Based Smart Evacuation System Applying Crowd Simulation and Active Evacuation Guidance (Open Innovation Type)

## Basic Information
- Demonstration company: iCaptain
- Demonstration year: 2024
- Support amount: KRW 25,000,000
- Location: 674 Gyeongin-ro, Namdong-gu, Incheon (Ganseok-dong)
- Demonstration partner: Incheon Transit Corporation
- Demonstration target:
  - 2 stations on Incheon Line 1 (Incheon City Hall Station, Dongsu Station)
  - 2 stations on Incheon Line 2 (Incheon Gajwa Station, Seongnam Station)
- Area: Basement levels 3 to 5
- Capacity: Approximately 2,400 people (during peak passenger hours)
- Usage: Transportation facility (station)
- Special note: For Dongmak Station and Incheon Line 1, 3D assets of tunnels and station exterior materials are available
- Category: Space

## Demonstration Overview
- Case name: AI-Based Smart Evacuation System Applying Crowd Simulation and Active Evacuation Guidance (Open Innovation Type)
- Purpose: To verify simulation technology and evacuation route calculation technology that can improve occupant evacuation safety during subway-station disaster situations.

## Demonstration Details
1. Because subway stations can cause large-scale casualties, the lack of evacuation-time analysis during disasters creates major difficulties in safety evaluation and response.
2. Conducted a demonstration by applying and verifying simulation technology to improve occupant evacuation safety in stations managed by Incheon Transit Corporation.

## Demonstration Objectives
1. 95% accuracy for the 3D subway station model
2. 100% accuracy for crowd and fire simulation
3. Disaster evacuation-route calculation time within 0.5 seconds
4. 100% accuracy of disaster evacuation-route calculation

## Demonstration Method
1. Developed 3D subway station models based on drawings
2. Conducted real-time optimal evacuation-route algorithms and crowd simulation
3. Tested simulation accuracy, including disaster evacuation calculation time and route calculation accuracy

## Demonstration Results
1. 100% achieved for subway station 3D model accuracy
2. 100% achieved for crowd and fire simulation accuracy
3. 100% achieved for disaster evacuation-route calculation time
4. 100% achieved for disaster evacuation-route calculation accuracy
